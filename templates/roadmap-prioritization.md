# Template: Roadmap Prioritization

## Suggested Topic

`What should <project> prioritize next, what order should work ship in, and what can be deferred?`

## Framing Prompts

- What user problem are we trying to unblock first?
- What must be true for effective dogfooding/adoption?
- Which items are correctness/safety gates vs polish?
- What can be deferred without harming learning?

## Divergence Prompts

- Propose a sequencing strategy and explain tradeoffs.
- Name the riskiest assumption in your ordering.
- Identify dependencies that force ordering.

## Proposal Skeleton

`Prioritize next work as: (0) correctness gates, (1) adoption unblockers, (2) workflow reliability, (3) UX polish, (4) advanced features deferred unless dogfood demand appears. Specific order: ...`

## Resolution Checklist

- Ordered list of items
- Explicit deferrals
- Ownership / review split (if known)
- Acceptance evidence for the top 1-2 items
